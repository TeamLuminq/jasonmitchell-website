How a Made-For-You build runs

Four to six weeks, end to end. Here's the shape of it.

1

Discover

A kick-off call to map your team's real workflows and rank them by impact. Who uses it, what a typical week looks like, where the time goes.

2

Plan

A delivery plan you sign off: every workflow, what I build, and what I need from you. Prioritised so the things that matter most get built first.

3

Build & test

Around five non-consecutive days of focused build. Context files first, then each skill built and tested against your real examples until it saves real time.

4

Handover

A live training session where your team uses it on real work, a setup guide they keep, and email support for any teething issues.

What your team gets

  • A full set of company context files in your voice
  • A prioritised set of custom skills for your workflows
  • Connectors to your key tools, documented
  • Any agreed scheduled tasks, set up and verified
  • A setup guide deck the team keeps
  • A live handover and training session
  • Email support
  • A clear note of anything deferred or handed back

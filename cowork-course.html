Real examples

The kinds of things you could build

A flavour of what's possible, drawn from real TV, video and creative workflows. Yours will be built around your own business.

Context files: the foundation

  • A brand & voice profile so everything sounds like you
  • Broadcaster house-style guides (BBC, C4, Netflix)
  • A contributor & crew database
  • Your slate of formats in development

Custom Skills: the workhorses

  • Treatment & pitch-deck writer
  • Call-sheet generator
  • Risk-assessment builder
  • Running order & shooting-script generator
  • Casting & contributor outreach
  • Budget & cost-report drafter
  • Edit-notes summariser from transcripts
  • Social caption & subtitle cleaner

Connectors & Plugins

  • Gmail/Outlook triage and on-voice replies
  • Drive/Dropbox to pull files and file deliverables
  • Calendar for shoot and hold dates
  • Slack/Teams team updates
  • Canva/Gamma/design tool connectors
  • YouTube/social analytics

Scheduled Tasks & Claude Design

  • A daily inbox & calendar brief each morning
  • A weekly production status digest
  • A monthly YouTube performance report
  • On-brand pitch slides
  • Thumbnail & title-card concepts
  • Social graphics & sell-sheets
The payoff

By the end of four weeks you'll have:

  • Cowork configured around your company, your voice and your files, so its output sounds like you from day one.
  • Your own custom Skills that automate the repetitive work you do every week.
  • Your key tools connected (email, calendar, Drive and more) through Connectors and Plugins.
  • Scheduled tasks running quietly in the background, doing work while you sleep.
  • The confidence to build new automations yourself, long after the course ends.
  • A recording of every session for catchup, and access to a network of other participants building in Cowork.
